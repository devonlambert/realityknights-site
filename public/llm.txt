# Reality Knights LLC

> A small holding company building products, platforms, and investments at the intersection of culture and technology.

Reality Knights LLC is a founder-led holding company based in Austin, TX. We build businesses that sit at the crossover of culture, creativity, and technology — focusing on communities and products that people are genuinely passionate about.

## Ventures

### Geeky Inc
Status: Active
URL: https://geekyinc.com
Description: An anime, gaming, and 3D printing brand. Geeky Inc makes collectible 3D-printed figures inspired by Japanese folklore, anime aesthetics, and video game culture. Products are sold via Shopify and Etsy. Content is published via Ghost CMS at geekyinc.com covering anime, RPG gaming, and 3D printing.

### Lootsignal
Status: Paused
URL: https://lootsignal.com
Description: An AI-powered real estate discovery platform that surfaces properties with high profit potential and uses machine learning to assess deal quality before an offer is made. Currently paused; not abandoned.
